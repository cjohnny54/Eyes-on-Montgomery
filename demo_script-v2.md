# Presentation Script: Montgomery — Eyes on our Community
**Objective:** Demonstrate how the civic intelligence platform transforms raw city data and public sentiment into actionable insights using live AI and real-time scraping.

---

## 1. Introduction (The Vision)
**Visual:** Dashboard landing page (Incident Map).

**Script:**
"Welcome to 'Montgomery — Eyes on our Community.' In a modern city, information moves faster than resources. Our goal was to create a single 'civic lens' that allows city leaders and residents to see exactly how Montgomery is performing in real-time. 

Today, we are looking at a platform that doesn't just show data—it thinks about it. This isn't just a map; it's a decision-support system powered by live AI to answer one critical question: **Where do we need to focus today?**"

---

## 2. District Incidents & AI Studio (The Predictive Core)
**Visual:** Incident Map → Navigating to **AI Studio**. Pointing out the **"LIVE AI"** badges.

**Script:**
"We start with the **AI Studio**, the cognitive engine of the platform. Using Gemini 3.1 Pro, the system analyzes thousands of 311 records to generate live reports.

**The Actionable Insight:** Notice the **'LIVE AI'** indicators. The system has identified that while District 3 has the highest volume of reports, the **Anomaly Detection** AI has flagged a 200% spike in 'Code Enforcement' issues in District 5. It’s also **Predictive**—forecasting that we will see a surge in pothole requests next week due to the recent weather. We are moving from **reactive maintenance** to **predictive planning**."

---

## 3. Montgomery Voice (Measuring Public Mood)
**Visual:** Switching to **Montgomery Voice**. Clicking "Analyze Sentiment".

**Script:**
"Civic intelligence isn't just about what's in our database; it's about what people are saying on the streets and online. This is **Montgomery Voice**. 

**The Actionable Insight:** In real-time, the platform uses **Bright Data Web Unblockers** to scrape Reddit, AL.com, and local forums. Gemini then analyzes the raw text to provide a **Sentiment Score**. If the score for 'Trash Pickup' drops from 70 to 45 in 24 hours, we know there is a systemic breakdown in communication or service before the first formal complaint even reaches the City Hall desk. We are listening to the community at scale."

---

## 4. 311 Service Requests & Responsiveness
**Visual:** Switching to the 311 Service Requests and City Responsiveness dashboards.

**Script:**
"Moving to **311 Service Requests & Responsiveness**, we see the execution phase. We track the 'SLA Gap'—the distance between when a resident asks for help and when the job is done.

**The Actionable Insight:** Data allows us to hold departments accountable. If 'Illegal Dumping' response times are lagging in District 2, we can investigate if it’s a staffing issue or equipment failure. We celebrate the departments exceeding expectations and optimize the ones that need support."

---

## 5. Traffic Hotspots (Proactive Policy)
**Visual:** Switching to the Traffic Hotspots dashboard.

**Script:**
"Finally, we look at **Traffic Hotspots**. By merging incident data with traffic safety reports, we define high-priority areas.

**The Actionable Insight:** Instead of waiting for a serious accident to happen, we look at the 'near-misses' and nuisance reports. This data guides where we place speed trailers, where we need more visible police presence, and where we should consider permanent infrastructure changes like speed humps or better signage."

---

## 6. Conclusion (The ROI of Data)
**Visual:** Returning to the main overview.

**Script:**
"Montgomery is a city on the move. By using this platform, we ensure that every dollar spent and every hour worked is backed by live intelligence. We aren't just managing a city; we are listening to it, predicting its needs, and responding with precision.

Data tells the story; AI illuminates the path; we take the action. Thank you."
