# Presentation Script: Montgomery — Eyes on our Community
**Objective:** Demonstrate how the civic intelligence platform transforms raw city data into actionable insights for municipal decision-making.

---

## 1. Introduction (The Vision)
**Visual:** Dashboard landing page (District Incidents).

**Script:**
"Welcome to 'Montgomery — Eyes on our Community.' In a modern city, information moves faster than resources. Our goal was to create a single 'civic lens' that allows city leaders, council members, and residents to see exactly how Montgomery is performing in real-time. 

This isn't just a map; it's a decision-support system designed to answer one critical question: **Where do we need to focus today?**"

---

## 2. District Incidents & Public Safety (Immediate Response)
**Visual:** Navigating the Incident Map and filtering by Councils/Districts.

**Script:**
"We start with the **Incident Map**. Historically, 311 calls and code violations were viewed in spreadsheets. Here, we've mapped them directly to City Council Districts.

**The Actionable Insight:** If a council member sees a cluster of abandoned vehicle reports or 'overgrown lot' violations in a specific neighborhood, they don't have to wait for a monthly report. We can see the density of issues immediately. By clicking into a district, we can prioritize code enforcement patrols to the specific blocks that are dragging down neighborhood safety and property values."

---

## 3. 311 Service Requests (Resource Allocation)
**Visual:** Switching to the 311 Service Requests dashboard.

**Script:**
"Moving to **311 Service Requests**, we transition from 'what' is happening to 'how' we allocate our budget. By analyzing service demand by neighborhood, we can see shifts in infrastructure needs. 

**The Actionable Insight:** If we notice a 20% spike in 'street light out' requests in the west side over the last quarter, that’s a data-driven signal to move maintenance crews or initiate a lighting audit. It helps us move from **reactive maintenance** to **predictive planning**."

---

## 4. City Responsiveness (Performance Accountability)
**Visual:** Switching to the City Responsiveness dashboard.

**Script:**
"Efficiency is our accountability metric. The **City Responsiveness** dashboard tracks how fast we actually resolve these issues. 

**The Actionable Insight:** We can see exactly which categories—like trash pickup or pothole repair—are meeting their SLAs and which are lagging. If responsiveness on 'illegal dumping' is slowing down, we can investigate if it’s a staffing issue, an equipment failure, or a seasonal surge. Data allows us to hold departments accountable and celebrate the ones exceeding expectations."

---

## 5. Traffic Hotspots (Proactive Policy)
**Visual:** Switching to the Traffic Hotspots dashboard.

**Script:**
"Finally, we look at **Traffic Hotspots**. By merging incident data with traffic safety reports, we define high-priority areas.

**The Actionable Insight:** Instead of waiting for a serious accident to happen, we look at the 'near-misses' and nuisance reports. This data guides where we place speed trailers, where we need more visible police presence, and where we should consider permanent infrastructure changes like speed humps or better signage."

---

## 6. Conclusion (The ROI of Data)
**Visual:** Returning to the main overview.

**Script:**
"Montgomery is a city on the move. By using this dashboard, we ensure that every dollar spent and every hour worked by city staff is backed by data. We focus on the areas that need us most, we solve problems faster, and we build a safer, more responsive Montgomery for everyone.

Data tells the story; we take the action. Thank you."
